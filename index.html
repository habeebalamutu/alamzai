About Alamz

Learn about our mission and the creator behind this innovative AI assistant

Our Mission

At Alamz, our mission is to democratize access to artificial intelligence, making powerful AI tools available to everyone regardless of technical expertise. We believe that AI should be an empowering tool that enhances human creativity and productivity.

Our Technology

We leverage state-of-the-art large language models combined with proprietary enhancements to deliver accurate, context-aware responses. Our system incorporates memory and context awareness to provide coherent, multi-turn conversations that feel natural and intuitive.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your billing will be prorated based on your current usage.

Do you offer discounts for students?

Yes, we offer a 50% discount for verified students. Contact our support team with your student ID for more information.

Is there a free trial?

All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start your trial.

How is billing handled?

We bill monthly on the date you signed up. You can cancel at any time with no cancellation fees.
